Government Schemes & Subsidies

Financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year for small and marginal farmers, provided in 3 equal installments.

Required Documents:
  • Aadhar Card
  • Land Ownership Records (Khatauni / 7/12)
  • Bank Account Passbook (Aadhar Linked)

Get up to 60% subsidy on installing solar water pumps for irrigation. This helps reduce reliance on grid electricity and diesel.

Required Documents:
  • Aadhar Card & Passport Size Photo
  • Land Records (Jamabandi / 7/12 Extract)
  • Bank Passbook Copy
  • Declaration of no existing electric connection

Up to 50-80% subsidy on buying new tractors, rotavators, and other modern farming equipment to promote agricultural mechanization.

Required Documents:
  • Aadhar Card & PAN Card
  • Quotation/Bill of the Machinery
  • Caste Certificate (If applicable for extra subsidy)
  • Bank Details & Land Records

About Us

Cultivating a Better Tomorrow, Together

What it is: GreenHarvest is a pioneering digital agriculture platform dedicated to revolutionizing the farming industry. Born from a deep respect for the land and the people who work it, our platform serves as a comprehensive ecosystem where traditional knowledge meets cutting-edge technology.

Who it is for: Our community is built for everyone involved in the agricultural supply chain. Whether you are a small-scale local farmer tending to a few acres, or a commercial enterprise, GreenHarvest provides the resources and support you need to thrive.

Why it is useful: By offering expert crop advisory, accessible heavy equipment rentals, and real-time market price tracking, we empower you to make data-driven decisions. Our goal is to help you maximize your profitability while maintaining sustainable farming practices.

Our Mission

To empower farmers globally by providing accessible technology, fair market insights, and sustainable farming solutions.
